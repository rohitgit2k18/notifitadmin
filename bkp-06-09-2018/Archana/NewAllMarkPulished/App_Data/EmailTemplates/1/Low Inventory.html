To Manage your Inventory:

Go to the Low Inventory Report to view and change all items meeting the low inventory stock level.

You can change the inventory options for a product from the Edit Product page.

  • Enable/Disable Inventory Tracking
  • Change Quantity in stock
  • Change the level at which you will start receiving email notifications
  • Enable/Disable Backorder for a product that is out of stock

You can change the store's global inventory settings from the Configure > Store page.

  • Enable/Disable Inventory management
  • Change the In-Stock message displayed to your customer
  • Change the Out-of-Stock message displayed to your customer
  • Enable/Disable inventory messages shown to your customer
